Taco Bomb

As a final project to a design class, I was assigned to make a logo for a new food truck called Taco Bomb based out of Albuquerque, New Mexico. To create a unique logo, I focused on bomb illustrations instead of taco illustrations. I wanted the logo to give off a southwestern vibe, and it's evident in the other logos I made. The theme for the final logo moved further south as I got inspiration from the Day of the Dead celebrations of Mexico.
