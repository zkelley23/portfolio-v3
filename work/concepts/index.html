UI Concepts

Overview

These designs are all concepts that I've created in my free time, with the exception of the BlueStep Youth website design which I made while working for BlueStep Systems.

Bank app

The bank app that I use isn't very user-friendly, and it lacks features that I would like. I created this bank app concept with features in mind. I designed an interface that made viewing money a more visual experience. I created an app that solved the pain points I had experienced using my bank's app.

Weather app

I've always been interested in the weather, so naturally, I've used many weather apps. Out of the different weather apps I have, none of them have all the features I want. I have to switch between apps to get all of the information I need. This weather app concept has all the numbers and data I would need, all in one place.

BlueStep Youth

BlueStep Systems created a new product for behavioral schools called BlueStep Youth, and I was asked to design the website. I created the design with potential clients in mind. I wanted to make a design that was clean and easy to understand to match the organization and simplicity of the product. The marketing team loved the design, and they appreciated its look and feel.

Travel site

I had seen many people designing travel site mock-ups, and I wanted to create one too. I wanted to create a unique interface. I am a visual person, so I like looking at maps when I am planning a trip. I designed the interface with a map as the main search feature. When a user clicks on a country, they can see important information about the country itself. They can also see some of the main tourist attractions that the country has to offer.

Utah Jazz site

As a fan of the Utah Jazz, I love looking at statistics from games and seeing how players performed. Most sports apps and websites show stats and player information in tables. I wanted to make a design that showed stats and info visually and dynamically to match the feel of the game.
